Excel: Managing and Analyzing Data (LinkedIn Qualification)

This section summaries the resources I used to learn Excel skills in preperation for my new role. It is important to note that there are many great courses online by many different authors, that cover the same material to an equally high standard, and that I have only listed the one I found most useful out of the ones I have completed in the interest of reducing the repetition of content. However I did complete many more courses as this offered explanations from different authors and different data sets. With this being said, I started with this course by Oz du Soleil

This course offers an incredibly comprehensive review into data management before any analysis can take place. This course places emphasis on the cleaning, accuracy and usefulness of the ‘raw’ data before moving further in the process and the importance this has. As such, it covers data cleansing tools and formulas, organising data from imported sources using dynamic arrays and power queries, as well as covering the management of multiple data sets with very thorough explanations of VLOOKUP and XLOOKUP as well as in Power Query.
